About BRAVE

BRAVE ATHLETES CLUB is a community-based sports and conditioning club focused on healthy movement, athletic development, and functional body care.

Our main activities are based in Osaka, with additional support available in Tokyo and Yokohama. In the Kanto area, we mainly provide personal support and selected group sessions.

We work with children, athletes, active adults, and people who want to move better, stay healthy, and enjoy sport safely.
