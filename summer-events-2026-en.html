Summer Conditioning & Speed Events

Build your movement foundation in July, then move into more practical speed, agility, and power work during summer break. These short programs are designed to help children and junior athletes move better for sport.

What You Can Find Here

This page gives an overview of our July conditioning clinic and two summer intensive programs. You can compare the purpose, target age, and schedule of each event.

Summer Flow

STEP 1

July | Build the Foundation

Improve posture, mobility, stability, balance, and movement control to prepare the body for efficient movement.

STEP 2

Summer Break | Apply It to Sport

Focus on first-step quickness, weight transfer, acceleration, stopping, turning, change of direction, and jumping power.

What We Value in Coaching

At Brave Athletes Club, we do not focus only on doing more repetitions or pushing children harder. We place importance on how the body moves, why a movement matters, and how to improve safely and effectively.

We adjust the intensity and difficulty according to age, experience, and physical condition so that each participant can learn with confidence.
