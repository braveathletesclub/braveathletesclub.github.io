What Brave Classes Value

Step-by-Step Training Based on Growth and Development

We support children and athletes according to their stage of physical development, helping them improve without rushing the process. Learning correct movement early creates a stronger base for long-term athletic growth.

Conditioning That Improves Movement Quality

We focus on the feet, posture, balance, and coordinated movement patterns to build a body that moves well and stays healthy. Our approach is never limited to “core training” alone.

Physical Development That Connects to Sport

We help young athletes develop correct form and better body control, then build speed, power, and stability step by step. Our goal is long-term athlete development, not short-term overload.

Ages 1–9

Kids Sports Class

Every Wednesday 16:00–17:30

Helping children enjoy movement and build confidence

  • Play-based movement experiences
  • Basic running, jumping, and throwing skills
  • Balance and coordination
  • Foundations for good posture and body control

This class is designed for young children to enjoy moving their bodies while building the foundations needed for future sport and daily movement.

Recommended for: children who love to move, children who need more confidence in exercise, and children who would benefit from improving posture and body awareness.

Ages 10–15

Junior Athlete Conditioning Class

Every Wednesday 16:20–17:50

Learn the conditioning young athletes truly need

  • Posture and movement improvement
  • Agility, balance, and coordination
  • Flexibility and stability development
  • Correct form and body control
  • Injury prevention and athletic foundations

This class is designed to build the physical foundation young athletes need, regardless of sport.

It also reflects the same core ideas used in our seasonal conditioning programs, helping participants improve movement quality while enjoying regular weekly training.

What Brave Values in This Class

Step-by-Step Guidance Based on Development

We coach according to each athlete’s stage of physical development, helping them improve movement quality safely and effectively.

Conditioning That Improves How the Body Moves

We improve the feet, posture, balance, and movement coordination so athletes can move more efficiently and stay healthier.

Physical Development That Connects to Sport

During the junior years, learning correct form and better body use creates the base for future speed, power, and performance.

Ages 14–18

Functional Training Class

For junior high and high school athletes

For student athletes aiming for higher performance

  • Speed, power, and agility development
  • More effective body use for sport
  • Movement and form improvement
  • Conditioning and injury prevention
  • Performance-focused training

This class builds on the movement-quality foundation developed in the junior program, then adds more advanced work for total-body power, speed, and higher performance.

It is recommended for student athletes who want more purposeful training to improve competitive performance.
