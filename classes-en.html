Conditioning Class to Improve Movement Quality

A 2-day conditioning program designed for junior athletes and active adults who want to build a stronger, more efficient body.

Through movement assessment and practical conditioning work, participants learn how to improve mobility, flexibility, balance, stability, and overall movement quality.

Who is this class for?

Junior Athletes

Recommended for athletes aged 7–18 who want to improve movement quality, body control, and athletic foundations.

Active Adults

Also suitable for adults who want to move better, train more efficiently, and build a healthier, stronger body.

What we focus on

Movement Assessment

We check mobility, flexibility, balance, agility, and overall movement patterns from multiple angles to better understand how each body moves.

Practical Conditioning

Participants work on trunk stability, stretching, body control, and type-based conditioning to improve movement quality and reduce injury risk.

2-Day Structure

Day 1 | Assessment & Foundations

  • Mobility and flexibility check
  • Balance and stability check
  • Simple stretching
  • Basic trunk and body control training

Day 2 | Conditioning Practice

  • Shared conditioning instruction
  • Type-based practical programming
  • Movement quality improvement
  • Foundational work for future training

Both days are required to complete the program.
