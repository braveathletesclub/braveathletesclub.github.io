Class Programs

From preschoolers to junior athletes, our class programs build solid movement foundations while keeping things fun and safe. We focus on motor development, posture, movement quality, and sport-based training.

Kids Sports Conditioning BB Spring Conditioning

Program Features

Motor Development

Running, jumping, throwing, balancing—fundamental movement skills that support all sports and daily activities.

Posture & Movement Quality

Improving alignment and movement patterns to reduce stress on the body and help prevent injury.

Fun & Sport-Based Approach

Agility, coordination, and game-style drills help children naturally build confidence and skills.

🌸 Spring Conditioning Class (2026)

A 2-day conditioning program during spring break designed to improve movement quality, body awareness, and athletic foundations.
Through a range of movement checks and practical exercises, participants learn how to build a stronger, more efficient body.

Day 1 | Assessment & Foundations

The first day focuses on checking mobility, flexibility, balance, agility, and overall movement patterns, followed by simple stretching and trunk stability work.

  • Date: Mon, Mar 30, 2026
  • Time: 13:00–14:30
  • Eligibility: Ages 7 and above

Day 2 | Conditioning Practice

On the second day, participants receive shared conditioning instruction together with type-based programming to help improve movement quality and body control.

  • Date: Tue, Mar 31, 2026
  • Time: 13:00–14:30
  • Fee: 4,500 JPY

Important Notes

  • Both days are required.
  • Brave members and athletes from partner organizations receive a 500 JPY discount.
  • This class is recommended for junior athletes as well as active adults who want to improve how they move.

🤝 Inclusive Support

We adapt pace, environment, and communication style so that children who need more time or support can participate safely and comfortably.
