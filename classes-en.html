What Makes Brave Classes Different

Age-Appropriate Progression

We support each participant according to their stage of growth and development, helping them improve step by step without rushing the process.

Movement Quality First

We focus on posture, balance, coordination, and body control so that children and athletes can build a strong and sustainable physical foundation.

Fun, Safe, and Professional

Our classes are enjoyable and welcoming, while still grounded in professional support for movement, conditioning, and athletic development.

Ages 1–9

🟩 Kids Sports Class

Every Wednesday 16:00–17:30

Helping children enjoy movement and build confidence

  • Play-based movement experiences
  • Basic running, jumping, and throwing skills
  • Balance and coordination
  • Foundations for good posture and body control

This class is designed for young children to enjoy moving their bodies while building the foundations needed for future sport and daily movement.

Recommended for: children who love to move, children who need more confidence in exercise, and children who would benefit from improving posture and body awareness.

Ages 10–15

🟦 Junior Athlete Conditioning Class

Every Wednesday 16:20–17:50

Building a stronger body for junior athletes

  • Posture and movement improvement
  • Agility and coordination
  • Core stability, balance, and flexibility
  • Technique support and injury prevention

This class is designed for junior athletes who want to improve the quality of their movement, develop a stronger athletic base, and build better control of the body.

It also supports the growing body during important developmental years by helping reduce movement inefficiencies and lowering the risk of avoidable injuries.

Ages 14–18

🟥 Functional Training Class

For junior high and high school athletes

For student athletes who want to improve performance

  • Learning how to use the body effectively for sport
  • Building a foundation for speed, power, and stability
  • Movement and form improvement
  • Injury prevention and ongoing conditioning

This class focuses on building a body that performs well in sport, not just a body that works hard. We work on stability, posture, coordination, force transfer, and movement efficiency.

It is recommended for student athletes who want more purposeful training to support better athletic performance.
