Recommended For

Who This Is For

  • Athletes or adults dealing with pain, tightness, or recurring issues
  • Those who want to refine technique and reduce injury risk
  • Women experiencing physical changes such as postpartum recovery or menopause
  • Anyone who prefers focused one-to-one support rather than group sessions
What We Do

Conditioning Based Personal Training

Personal sessions at Brave are based on movement assessment and conditioning, not only strength training.

  • Movement assessment
  • Mobility & flexibility
  • Core stability
  • Posture correction
  • Balance & coordination
  • Functional strength
  • Injury prevention
  • Rehabilitation support
  • Women's conditioning
  • Sport-specific training

Each program is adjusted individually based on age, sport, and physical condition.

Session Flow

How Personal Training Works

  • Step 1 Interview & movement check
  • Step 2 Assessment of posture, mobility, balance
  • Step 3 Individual conditioning program
  • Step 4 Training & correction
  • Step 5 Follow-up and progression

Sessions are designed to support long-term improvement, not just short-term training.
