Group & Team Support

Support Options

Small Group

Small Group Lessons

Lessons for companies, organizations, local groups, and small teams. As a general guideline, one instructor works with up to six participants.

We support posture, movement quality, balance, stability, and conditioning habits that can be continued safely.

Parent & Child

Parent-Child Group Lessons

Parent-child sessions focus on enjoyable movement experiences such as running, jumping, balancing, throwing, and body control.

These sessions are generally designed for up to six parent-child pairs.

Team Support

Team Conditioning Support

Conditioning support for high school teams, university teams, club teams, and sports organizations.

Team sessions are planned separately based on the number of participants, session length, training period, and competition demands.

Service Area

We are mainly based in Osaka and Takatsuki. We also accept consultations for sessions in Tokyo, Kanagawa, and other selected areas in the Kanto region.

Parent-child lessons in the Shibuya area and small group lessons in Yokohama may also be available depending on schedule and venue conditions.

* Please contact us to discuss location, venue conditions, and travel arrangements.
