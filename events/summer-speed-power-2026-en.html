Summer Program 2026

Speed & Power-Up Training

A 2-day summer intensive training program for junior athletes to improve first-step quickness, acceleration, change of direction, jumping ability, and lower-body power.

Ages 11–16

Develop Speed and Power for Sport

Speed in sport is not only about running fast in a straight line. It also involves weight transfer, acceleration mechanics, stopping, change of direction, jumping, and force production.

This program helps junior athletes build movement skills that transfer across many sports, including baseball, soccer, basketball, swimming, and track and field.

Recommended For Athletes Who…

Want a quicker first step

Improve how you transfer body weight and move forward from the very first step.

Want better acceleration

Learn how posture, foot contact, and body position affect efficient acceleration.

Want to improve change of direction

Practice stopping, turning, and re-accelerating with better control and efficiency.

Want more jumping power

Develop lower-body power and landing control in a safe and structured way.

Main Program Content

Day 1

First Step, Acceleration, and Speed

Movement checks, posture and footwork preparation, start drills, and short acceleration work.

Day 2

Deceleration, Change of Direction, and Power

Stopping mechanics, agility drills, re-acceleration, jumping work, and power development.

Coaching Focus

Quality Before Quantity

We focus on safe and efficient movement quality rather than simply increasing volume or intensity.
