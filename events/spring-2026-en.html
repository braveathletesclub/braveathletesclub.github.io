Spring Special Programs

Spring Events 2026

During the spring break season, Brave Athletes Club offers special programs for children, athletes, and adults.
From family-friendly activities to practical conditioning and mindful movement classes, our spring events combine fun, body awareness, and movement quality.

BRAVE Featured Program

Conditioning Class to Improve Movement Quality

Mar 30 (Mon) & Mar 31 (Tue), 2026 | 13:00–14:30 | Takatsuki City General Gymnasium, Sub Gym 2

A 2-day conditioning class designed to improve mobility, flexibility, balance, agility, and overall movement quality.

This program is especially recommended for junior athletes, but it is also suitable for active adults who want to move better and build a stronger, more efficient body.

  • Ages 7 and above
  • Fee: 4,500 JPY
  • 500 JPY discount for Brave members and partner athletes
  • Both days are required

Indoor Sports Festival

Mar 29 (Sun), 2026 | 13:00–14:30 | Takatsuki City General Gymnasium, Sub Gym 2

A fun recreational sports event for children and adults. Families, friends, and individual participants are all welcome.

Activities may include tag games, obstacle races, relay events, rope jumping, balloon volleyball, and other playful movement-based challenges.

  • Ages 4 and above
  • Fee: 1,500 JPY per person
  • Family participation welcome

Yoga Class & Tai Chi Class

Yoga: Mar 29 (Sun), 15:00–16:00 | Tai Chi: Apr 1 (Wed), 15:00–16:00 | Takatsuki City General Gymnasium, Sub Gym 2
