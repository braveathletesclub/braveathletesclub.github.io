About BRAVE ATHLETES CLUB

About the Club

BRAVE ATHLETES CLUB is a community-based club that supports growth through movement – from kids who are confident in sports to those who feel a bit hesitant, as well as adults and athletes. We provide personalized support from posture & muscle function to performance enhancement and functional recovery.

What We Do

At BRAVE, we focus on:

  • ▶ Kids sports classes that turn “I’m not good at sports” into “I can do this a bit more!”
  • ▶ Personal conditioning for adults & athletes (posture, muscle function, performance, and recovery)
  • ▶ Inclusive environments where people with disabilities can enjoy moving together

Regardless of experience or level, we aim to help each person take one step forward from where they are now.

Training Approach

  • ● Building a solid foundation: improving posture and movement patterns first
  • ● Step-by-step progress tailored to each individual
  • ● Emphasis on positive feedback and “small successes”
  • ● Safety first, while creating an enjoyable and encouraging environment

Who It’s For

  • ・Kids & juniors (preschool to elementary)
  • ・Teens and adults
  • ・Athletes aiming to improve performance
  • ・Those recovering from injury or wanting easier daily movement
  • ・People seeking inclusive and para-sport friendly support

Main Services

  • ・Kids sports classes
  • ・Seasonal intensive programs and events
  • ・Personal training (60 / 75 minutes)
  • ・Functional recovery programs
  • ・On-site support for teams and schools
